Teacher Center Dashboard
Click the Menu Icon to open the site navigation menu.
Teacher Center Dashboard
The navigation menu displays all the site’s content.  To hide the menu, click the Menu Icon again.
Click eBook to view the currently selected lesson in the Teacher’s Edition. The Teacher’s Edition provides the lesson’s content and suggested answers.
To access resources for any chapter or lesson, scroll through the Lesson Resources carousel and click on the desired resource.
Preview scheduled lesson plans and assignments for each class in this section.
To access online help or log out, click the User Icon.
To search content by topic or standard, click the Search Icon. 
Click Chapter/Lesson to open a dropdown menu. 
Review the Chapter/Lesson drop down menu and click the lesson you’d like to review. In this example, we will click Lesson 2: Sequences under Chapter 5: Expressions.
Click the Menu Icon to display the site navigation menu.
Click Plan and Present to access instructional content.
The Plan and Present window displays instructional content, gives you access to ready-to-use lessons, and allows you to customize pre-built lessons with your own resources.
Teacher Center Plan and Present
Teacher Center Plan and Present
Lesson Planning tools allow you to add specific lessons to your teaching calendar, customize & create lesson plans for the chapter and lessons, and print lessons to PDF for offline learning.
The Presentation button accesses presentations that walk you through the lesson.
Recommended lesson plans from McGraw-Hill Education are highlighted. 
Lesson-reinforcing resources are available next to each lesson plan.
Click on a resource’s Gear Icon to display a menu of options.
Click Assign this Resource.
Teacher Center Assign
The Assignment Name and Instructions field allows you to name an assignment and provide instructions. If you are assigning LearnSmart, you can include instructions about what students will Read, Practice, or Recharge(review). You can also identify specific students for assignments and choose when assignments will expire
The Make an Assignment screen displays with the resource located on the right-hand side of the page in the Attach Resources and Content field.
To add an additional resource to a homework assignment, click Edit in the Attach Resources and Content field.
Use this field to select students that will receive assignments and send out student assignment notifications.
Teacher Center Assign
The Edit Resources and Content screen displays. Click the Add button under a resource to add it to the assignment. In this example, we are clicking Add to attach LearnSmart®, an adaptive teaching and learning tool, to the assignment.
When you are finished adding assignment resources, click Done.
Teacher Center Resources
Click Resources to open the Resources screen and view resources organized by chapter and lesson.
Click Keyword Search to search for resources. Click the star under a resource to add it to the Favorites tab  that is adjacent to the Keyword Search tab. 
Teacher Center Resources
Click the Menu Icon to open the site navigation menu.
Content is listed on the Lesson Search tab. Chapter/lesson dropdown menus located on the tab can be used to filter by specific resource type.

ConnectED Tech Tips
Before you start your digital review, there are a few things to take care of:
• Disable pop-up blockers; some pieces of interactivity will open in a new window • Un-mute your device • If possible, use a hard-wired connection to the internet • Close unnecessary applications (they can slow down your machine) • This review site is not mobile-friendly; please view on a laptop or desktop
